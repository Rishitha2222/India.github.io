About India


India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by area, the most populous country in the world, and the most populous democracy.

Fun Facts about India

India is the seventh largest country, despite being three times smaller than the U.S.

India has the largest democracy in the world, with 1 billion people and counting.

India was the first country to mine diamonds and it introduced shampoo to the world.

India produces 71.8% of the world's spices and was the first country to produce sugar.

The most popular sport in India os cricket, even though its national game is hockey.

Rajastan,one of India's 29 states, has a temple of rats.
